Staff Conference 2019

Staff Conference 2019

On-Board


Learning is a journey, not a destination

The theme for this year’s Staff Conference is ‘On Board’ because we believe that learning is a journey and not a destination and we are all on board on this journey to embrace lifelong learning.

Our three key learning areas in 2019 are Innovation, ICT and Assessment. For Innovation, we have been looking at adopting various innovative pedagogies in our Teaching and Learning. This has gone hand in hand with our ICT initiatives where we have utilized SLS as well as other online platforms to enhance the joy of learning for our digital natives. In terms of Assessment, we have been using holistic assessment to assess the effectiveness of these initiatives. Focusing on these 3 areas has helped us to develop the 21st century competencies in our pupils.
